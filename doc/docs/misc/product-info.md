# Product Information

Comprehensive information about Takazudo Modular's modular synthesizer case products.

## Product Type

Takazudo Modular cases are **DIY assembly kits** that consist of pre-cut panels and hardware components. These are not pre-assembled products - customers receive all necessary parts and assemble the case themselves following web-based assembly guides.

## What's Included

Each kit contains:
- Pre-cut panels (3D printed or acrylic laser cut)
- Rails (choice of 4 types)
- Hardware components (screws, washers, nuts, brackets)
- Access to web-based assembly guide

**What's NOT included:**
- Power supply (must be purchased separately)
- Pre-assembled case
- Physical assembly instructions (available online only)

## Panel Materials

Two panel material options are available:

### 3D Printed Panels
- Lightweight construction
- Default color: Carbon Black (カーボンブラック)
- Cost-effective option
- Suitable for portable setups

### Acrylic Laser Cut Panels
- More robust construction
- Default color: Glass Cyan (ガラスシアン)
- Premium feel with transparency
- Higher price point

**Note:** Aluminum panels are NOT available.

## Rail Options

Four rail types available, offering different material combinations and price points:

1. **Lite** - Most affordable option
2. **Nuts** - Recommended by Takazudo (推奨)
3. **Dual** - Mid-range option
4. **Metal** - Premium option

For detailed pricing information, see: https://panels.takazudomodular.com/price

## Case Sizes

Two size options available:

- **Small Type (40HP)** - Suitable for compact systems
- **Large Type (60HP)** - Better for expandable systems

## Product Limitations

### Durability Considerations
- **Not as tough as metal/wood cases** - Traditional metal or wooden cases are significantly more robust
- **Portability concerns** - Risk of damage when transporting outside home/studio
- **Material characteristics**:
  - PLA-CF (3D printed): Plastic-based, though relatively durable
  - Acrylic panels: Can crack or break with impact
- **Best for stationary use** - Primarily designed for in-home/in-studio setup
- **Can be moved** - Portable within reason, but requires careful handling

### Recommended Use Cases
- **Primary use**: Home studio or fixed installation
- **Secondary use**: Careful transport for performances or sessions (with proper protection)
- **Not recommended**: Frequent travel, touring, rough handling environments
